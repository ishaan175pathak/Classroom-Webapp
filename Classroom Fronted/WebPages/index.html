General Overview

We are a school with a big heart where personal relationships between students, teachers, staff, and parents are paramount. From our youngest learners through twelfth grade, each child is seen, heard, and valued by our community who knows them well. We pride ourselves on being a school for the whole family and are fortunate that parents play an integral role in a wide range of community activities. Families find myriad ways to support one another and the school during their time at At our School. In that same spirit of belonging, we welcome you to our website. We look forward to welcoming you on campus soon.
